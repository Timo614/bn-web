A new level of control

Our goal is for Big Neon to eventually issue tickets on the Tari blockchain. By doing so, we hope to eliminate counterfeit tickets, and enable promoters and venue owners to directly participate when their tickets are resold.

We lead from experience

Our founding team has deep experience building the most successful ticketing companies over the last 20 years.

Mobile-first

Buying tickets to see your favorite band shouldn’t be a painful experience. Big Neon is beautiful, easy-to-use, and is designed from the ground up to be mobile-first.

Open source

Big Neon is built in Rust and is licensed under BSD 3-Clause. Freely use components of the Big Neon codebase for your own projects.
